Where I'm Coming From


Ever since I was 10, I would always be lost in thought, trying to find my purpose. I would always hear people say "Everyone is meant to contribute to the world and solve a problem." Well, I didn't know what that problem was and I wanted, so badly to find out.

I discovered minimalism at the age of 15 and later embraced it at the age of 17. It was my first big step in my journey to a fulfilled, happy life.
"Happiness is not about having more. It is found in the ability to live comfortably with less."
Minimalism really cleared a path for me. It made me see what I was doing and to declutter my headspace. I felt great, but my journey had just started.

I later ventured into self-improvement videos, books, podcasts and anything else you can think of. My reading speed went from reading a 300 page book in 6 months to being able to read a total of 1,500 pages a day. I flew threw all kinds of books, ranging from 'Rich Dad, Poor Dad' to 'How to Win Friends and Influence People
